Eggcake! :D

Great for a easy meal in the morning or a simple dinner. The Frittata! You know, I personally called these eggcakes before I realized I'd been making a frittata all along. And I didn't figure that out as I was putting together this page. No sir, I did not. These can be made as simply or as complex as you want. Go crazy.

It recquires eggs. Surprise. Maybe some cream, some salt and pepper and maybe some other crazy seasonings. You can cut up meat or veggies as well. Think of it like a flat omelette. Just throw whatever you have at it. It'll likely turn out quite alright.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • dash of salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp cream or H&H
  • cheese to sprinkle on top
  1. Whish the eggs with the salt, pepper, and cream/H&H
  2. Put the skillet on low heat and pour in the mixture
  3. Cover with a lid and leave for five minutes. If the cake starts rising, it's done.
  4. Turn off the heat and place your cheese on top. Cover again and leave until melted.

This is the most basic version. You can add whatever you like to the initial mixture. Bacon, pork, chopped onion, shredded potato, etc. The eggcake is your oyster. I think.
