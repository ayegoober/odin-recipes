Oatmeal

A great way to start the day as you mull over where to strike lightning or what woman to chase after. It warms your heart and belly and puts a smile on your face.

You'll place a half cup of oatmeal in a bowl with 1 cup of water, then microwave for two minutes. If you burn hands upon removing the bowl, you've done well. You'll have wanted to chop of some apples while awaiting your scalding bowl. You will drop these in (they should be small dices) along with a tbsp of brown sugar, 2 tbsp of half and half and a dash of salt.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oatmeal
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup diced apples
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp half and half
  • dash of salt
  1. Mix water and oatmeal - microwave for two minutes
  2. mix in apples, half and half, brown sugar and salt.
  3. Allow to cool then enjoy. May add more half and half more creamier texture.
